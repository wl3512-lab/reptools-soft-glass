## 1. Features of Major Rep Tool Sites

### RepArchive / JadeShip (reparchive.com / jadeship.com)
- **Established**: 2017, operated by JadeShip
- **Tech Stack**: WordPress, jQuery, PHP, Ubuntu, Google Analytics
- **Key Tools**:
  - **Shipping Calculator**: Combines all agent shipping calculators on a single page for comparison; uncovers hidden fees for fair comparisons
  - **Spreadsheet Search**: Indexes 72+ Pandabuy spreadsheets (28,261+ rows) with a vector search engine
  - **Universal Link Converter**: Converts/sanitizes links from Taobao, Weidian, Tmall, 1688, and 25+ agent platforms (Pandabuy, Kakobuy, Hipobuy, MuleBuy, Superbuy, Sugargoo, Cssbuy, BaseTao, CnFans, HooBuy, AllChinaBuy, PonyBuy, OopBuy, ACBuy, and more)
  - **Bulk Link Converter**: Batch conversion of multiple links at once
  - **Best Selling Items**: Live feed of current purchases with time frames (today, this week, this month, all time) plus historical archive
  - **Seller Contacts**: Largest contact list with 489+ seller contacts with various contact methods
  - **Declaration Calculator**: Helps calculate customs declaration values
  - **Exchange Rate Tool**: Currency conversion
  - **Welcome Bonus Calculator**: Calculates sign-up bonuses across agents
  - **Agent Comparison**: Compares 12 agents across 21 aspects
  - **Native Search**: Searches Taobao, Weidian, and 1688 simultaneously (hybrid search)
  - **Finds Database**: 111,200+ finds (24x larger than the largest Pandabuy spreadsheet), with 11,100+ reviews
- **Browser Extension** (Chrome + Firefox): Converts links on Reddit and Yupoo to preferred agent; shows QC photo availability; handles shortened links; complete UI overhaul in June 2025
- **Design Philosophy**: Agent-agnostic by design

## 6. SEO Strategies for Rep Content Sites

### Content Strategy
- **Long-tail keywords**: Make up 91.8% of Google searches and 70% of search traffic
- Target specific queries: brand + item + "best batch", "W2C [brand] [item]", "[item] QC review"
- Deep expert content in niche areas beats large generalist sites
- Build topic clusters around specific product categories
- Trend-based articles attract editorial links

### Technical SEO (2025-2026)
- **Core Web Vitals**: Non-negotiable for ranking in 2026
- Page speed, mobile-friendliness, smooth navigation directly tied to ranking
- Google's January 2026 "Authenticity Update" prefers specialized, in-depth information
- 4 Core Updates in 2025 alone; staying current is critical

### E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness)
- Google values content quality and expertise over site size
- "Behind the Scenes" content builds trust
- User-generated content (reviews, QC assessments) adds authenticity
- Community engagement signals expertise

### Image SEO
- Product pages compete in Google Lens, Pinterest search, and image carousels
- QC photo galleries create large, indexable image libraries
- Alt text optimization for product images

### Link Building
- Trend-based articles and editorial content earn natural backlinks
- Community tools (calculators, converters) attract links as resources
- Cross-linking between tools, guides, and finds

- **Primary model**: Earn commission when users purchase through agent affiliate links
- Commission is based on international shipping fees, not item cost
- Pandabuy affiliate program: Free to join, commission on new registrations + purchases

- Bonus settled after parcel delivery, available for withdrawal

- Link converters generate affiliate-tagged links by default
- Every agent link on rep sites is typically an affiliate link

- Rep tool sites earn from the freight forwarding function

- Google AdSense or alternatives for banner/display ads

## 9. Shipping Calculators

### How They Work
1. **Input**: User enters weight (grams), dimensions (L x W x H in cm), and destination country
2. **Volumetric Weight Calculation**: (Length x Width x Height) / Divisor
   - DHL divisor: 5000 (1m3 = 200 kg)
   - Other carriers vary (5000-6000 range)
3. **Chargeable Weight**: MAX(actual weight, volumetric weight)
4. **Rate Lookup**: Apply carrier-specific rate tables based on destination and weight tier
5. **Output**: Estimated shipping cost in USD/local currency

### Shipping Lines & Typical Speeds (China to US)

| Line | Speed | Cost Level | Notes |
|------|-------|-----------|-------|
| DHL Express | 3-7 days | $$$ | Fastest, most reliable |
| EMS | 5-7 days | $$ | Good balance of speed/cost, up to 40% off official rates |

| China Post | 2-8 weeks | $ | Cheapest, slowest |

## 10. Size Conversion Tools (Chinese to US/EU)

### Key Sizing Differences
- Chinese sizes run significantly smaller than Western sizes
- Men's: China Large ≈ US Small
- Women's: China Large ≈ US Medium
- Best practice: Use body measurements (cm) rather than size labels

### Chinese Shoe Size Formula
```
Chinese Size = (Foot Length in CM x 2) - 10
Example: 25cm foot = (25 x 2) - 10 = Size 40
```
